About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the positive impact we're creating in the tech community.

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) at IIT is a dynamic student-led organization committed to empowering women in technology, engineering, and STEM fields. As part of the global IEEE WIE community, we aim to foster an inclusive environment where students can grow, innovate, and lead. Our mission is to inspire and support the next generation of women in STEM by providing opportunities for skill development, mentorship, and networking. Through interactive workshops, technical training, hackathons, and outreach programs, we encourage students to expand their knowledge and connect with industry professionals. We also organize leadership programs and community initiatives to promote gender diversity and bridge the gap in STEM. By collaborating with like-minded individuals and organizations, IEEE WIE at IIT continues to make a meaningful impact, empowering students to excel in their careers and contribute to the future of technology.
