SHErlock 2.0

The Ultimate Problem-Solving Challenge

On February 8th, 2025, the IEEE Women in Engineering Student Branch Affinity Group of IIT once again brought the excitement with SHErlock 2.0, marking its triumphant return as a highly anticipated problem-solving competition.

Building on the success of its previous run, SHErlock 2.0 pushed the boundaries even further, challenging undergraduates from across Sri Lanka to crack complex puzzles, unravel mysteries, and put their strategic thinking to the test. With immersive storytelling and high-stakes challenges, the competition demanded quick thinking, teamwork, and an unyielding determination to solve the toughest scenarios.

More than just a competition, SHErlock 2.0 was a journey of discovery, fostering critical thinking, teamwork, and a deeper appreciation for the groundbreaking work of women who have shaped STEM fields.
