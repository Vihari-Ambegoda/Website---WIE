SyncUp

The Power of Mentorship in Motion

SyncUp, the flagship mentorship initiative by IEEE WIE IIT, lit up the week with inspiration, growth, and powerful connections, bringing together 150+ passionate participants.

The journey began with the SyncUp Induction on the August 3rd. It was followed by SyncUp Accelerator on 5th, a power-packed session featuring thought-provoking keynotes from Shanthi Rajesh on engineering-to-leadership journeys, Dilshara Hetti Arachchige on building revenue-driven products, and Anderson Kirubaharan on embracing entrepreneurship as undergraduates. Fun-filled activities like Human Bingo, interactive real-talk sessions, and exciting gift rewards kept spirits soaring throughout the day.

The excitement carried into the SyncUp Finale on August 8th, where mentees engaged in 1:1 mentorship sessions with seniors, speed mentoring rounds, reflection sharing and thrilling mentor-mentee challenges with amazing prizes.

From inspiring keynotes to hands-on mentorship and vibrant activities, SyncUp wasn’t just an event; it was a movement. A platform where students discovered clarity, confidence, and community to accelerate their personal and professional journeys.
